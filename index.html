ABOUT US

Silverline Health is committed to providing high quality products and services within the healthcare space. Our focus is simple; to deliver reliable solutions in an ever-changing field and ensuring that the needs of providers and patients are met in an ethical manner.

Silverline Health was founded in 2020 by our CEO, Nusrat Jamal, in response to the various shortages and issues that became apparent during the Coronavirus pandemic. His emphasis is on being able to give back to the community and to address issues within the healthcare space as they arise.

Silverline Health is focused on employing locally, providing jobs and opportunity to Americans.
